Food

I love food a lot, as it's one of the things neccesary for being alive. I love all sorts of food, but my favorite by far has to be banana pudding. Technically it's a dessert and not a "food" in the typical sense but I can't think of any other foods on the top of my head that make me feel the same way banana pudding does. Me and my family specifically follow the recipe that comes on the side of Nilla Wafer boxes and make our own pudding, of course. It just doesn't taste the same if you use instant pudding.
