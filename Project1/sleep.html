Sleep

Sleep is great, I enjoy being able to do the thing that the body requires of me for several hours at a time a night. Sometimes, I do it even when it ISN'T night. My brain and body decide when I fall asleep which sometimes sucks because I'll end up sleeping at like 3 AM but it's fine because I still enjoy going to sleep. And being able to enjoy that is great, because I quite literally cannot live without sleep.
