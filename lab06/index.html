S'mores Chocolate Mug Cake

Made by Amy of Pitchforkfoodie

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cups flour
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • a dash of salt
  • 1/4 cups milk
  • a splash of vanilla
  • 3 tablespoons mini marshmallows, divided
  • 3 tablespoons chocolate chips or chocolate candy bar, divided
  • 1 graham cracker, divided
  • Optional: hot fudge sauce
  • Optional: whipped cream or ice cream

Instructions:

  1. Choose a microwavable mug. I like mugs that are a little bit shorter and wider rather than a tall skinny mug. Make sure it's big enough to hold at least 8 ounces.
  2. Add flour, sugar, baking powder, cocoa, and salt to the mug. Stir.
  3. Add milk, oil, and vanilla to the dry ingredients in the mug. Stir.
  4. Add 2 tablespoons of the chocolate chips or chocolate candy bar and 2 tablespoons of the marshmallows. Roughly crunch half of the graham cracker and add it to the mug. Stir.
  5. Microwave for 40 seconds. All microwaves cook a little differently, so cooking times may vary. Since I prefer my cake a little on the underdone side instead of the overdone side, I recommend starting with a shorter cooking time. I started with 40 seconds and it was just right. You can always cook the mug cake a little bit longer; I would cook it in 10-second intervals until you get the desired doneness. Remember that in the next step, you will be cooking the mug cake for an additional 10 seconds. DON'T OVERCOOK!
  6. Sprinkle the remaining graham cracker, marshmallows, and chocolate over the mug cake. Microwave for an additional 10 seconds.
  7. Remove from the microwave. Let it set for a minute. Drizzle with hot fudge sauce and top with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream if desired. Serve warm!
